Why Onam Matters

Onam is more than just a festival; it is the heartbeat of Kerala. It celebrates the Return of King Mahabali, a period in history known for perfect equality, prosperity, and justice.

It marks the end of the monsoon and the beginning of the harvest, symbolizing a new cycle of life. It transcends religion, bringing people of all faiths together to celebrate the "Unity of Man."
